Arrangement pour guitare de Jean François Delcamp - http://www.delcamp.net

tab last updated 3rd May 2005 - corrections to weed@wussu.com

******************************************************************************
left hand fingering added by WAV (19 August 2005) - vwamenel@yahoo.fr
this is how I play it, some stretches (measures 8 to 10) may be difficult for
small hands - alternative fingerings are in the form a/b
trills (indicated by fingerings in the form a+b) placed according to a CD
version by René Bartoli. There's one on the A in measure 10 but I can't find a
way to put it while maintaining the F in the bass.
