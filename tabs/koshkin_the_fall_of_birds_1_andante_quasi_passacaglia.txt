Fall of Birds: 1. Andante quasi Passacaglia
by Nikita Koshkin
Tablature by Russell Cook (astropig7@gmail.com)

The introduction is written in two voices: ordinato and pizzicato. The bass
notes are played pizzicato, while the treble notes are played naturally. I've
tried to make the distinction clear by marking the treble notes as held.

There is a section of chords where the bass notes are strummed with the thumb
and the treble notes are plucked with the other fingers. This is as indicated
on the sheet music and may take some practice.

All artificial harmonics are octave harmonics.

Tempo: Andante
Key: E minor
Tuning: Drop-D (DADGBE)
