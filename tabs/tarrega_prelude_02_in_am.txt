Notes:

Here's a nifty little prelude by Tarrega that is quite easy 
to learn. This is a very Spanish piece, which should not be
played at all so mathematically as the regularity of the tab
might suggest. The version played by Angel Romero is so full
of fermatas everywhere that I didn't even try to put them all
in - you must hear it performed. The left-hand fingering is
just a suggestion (it's how I play it).

Comments and corrections welcome!
