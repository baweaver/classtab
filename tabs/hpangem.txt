A New Ground - Henry Purcell (1659-1695)

Tuning: E A D G B E   -   Time Signature: 2/2

A lovely little baroque piece with tons of feeling. Somewhat tricky to
make it sound flawless but with good acoustics and a good grasp on those
hammer-ons and pull-offs it sounds wonderful. I purposely avoided using
open strings in many sections for better tone opportunities. Choose
whatever fingerings and note placements you find best.
