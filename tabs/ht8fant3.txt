            Title: Fantasia No.3
           Author: King Henry VIII "Tudor"  (1491 - 1547 / Renaissance)
   Transcribed by: Adam Guzowski (guitarra@poczta.fm)
             Date: 15-X-2002
Other corrections: (...are welcome)

1) Guitar tuning: (standard) EAGDBE - This one, I love to play with Capo on 2nd

2) General Info: Here is another beautiful lute work. This one, like "If love
                 now reigned", is not a music miniature, but a full composition
                 in five parts, some of which are difficult to play. Before
                 playing it, practice your Barre chords well, and listen to the
                 MIDI. Learn it slowly, very slowly. Compare it to "fantasias"
                 written by other composes. As in "If love now reigned", all
                 the parts are different and need to be played with different
                 emotions. Please mail me if you have problems with this or with
                 other compositions by Henry VIII. I'll try to help. Good luck.
      
3) Tabulature info: 
    
   1 2 3 4   ->  left hand fingering
   (I).....  ->  Hold BARRE Chord
   Arp.      ->  Arpeggio - all the chords can be played with soft arpeggio
   =====     ->  16th notes
    .        ->  Dotted note
   .....     ->  hold "this" note / notes / chord through all the dots

[NOTE - the MIDI, like the tab, is written in 6/8, but the main beats are not
always on the first note of the measures - weed@wussu.com - 6 November 2002]


Tempo:  6/8  (aprox. 100 bpm - but different bpm for different parts !!!)
