            Title: Pavane No.6 (from "Seis Pavanes da el Maestro")
           Author: Luis de Milan  (1500 - 1561 / Renaissance)
   Transcribed by: Adam Guzowski (guitarra@poczta.fm)
             Date: 16-XII-2002
Other corrections: (...are welcome)

1) Guitar tuning: (standard) EAGDBE - Old lute music sounds good with Capo
                                      on 3rd or 4th fret

2) General Info: I love this one. It is fast, and good fingering is 
                 very important. This is the 6th and last pavane from
                 Luis de Milan's "Sei Pavane da el Maestro" first published
                 in 1535-1536 in Valencia, as a part of a first method-book
                 for string instruments "Libro de Musica de vihuela de mano,
                 intitulando El maestro".(Book of compositions for vihuela 
                 'de mano' named MASTER). The First part of this book, is for
                 beginners, and includes '22 fantasias' and these '6 pavanas'.
                 The second part includes '18 fantasias', '4 tientos' and
                 'many, many songs'. Also in this book there is informations
                 about how to tune the vihuela, how to place the fingers etc.
                 The first guitar player, who (hundreds of years later)
                 transcribed some parts of this book was Andres Segovia.
                 That's all. :)  Listen to the MIDI... Good luck ! :)
      
3) Tablature info:

   (I)...      -> Hold BARRE Chord (on exp. 1st fret)
   Arp         -> Arpeggio
   ,,,,,,      -> Try to hold bass note as long as possible (to the end of ,,,)
