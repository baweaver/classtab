Sonata in Em D566, I - Moderato, by Franz Schubert
**************************************************

My transcription of this beautiful piano sonata from Schubert. Arranged
to be playable on guitar whilst keeping the original feel as much
as possible. Requires some practice to get the hang of the middle part.
By Maxime Bergeron (mazout360@hotmail.com)
