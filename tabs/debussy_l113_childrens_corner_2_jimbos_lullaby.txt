Notes and Legend
----------------

This work describes an elephant, Jumbo, who came from the French Sudan and
lived briefly in the Jardin des Plantes in Paris around the time of Debussy's
birth. It is a beautiful lullaby with some dark moments and whole-tone passages
in the middle.

W  - whole note
H  - half note
Q  - quarter note
E  - 8th note
S  - 16th note
.  - note dotted (note held for an extra half length) 

pp   - pianissimo (very soft)
ppp  - pianissimo (extremely soft)

NH   - natural harmonic
<3>  - natural harmonic with the left hand finger pointing to the 3rd fret
<5>  - natural harmonic with the left hand finger pointing to the 5th fret
<7>  - natural harmonic with the left hand finger pointing to the 7th fret

L - tied note (continue holding the previous note)
