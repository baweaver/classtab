Elogio de la Danza  (1964) 
Leo Brouwer  (Havana, 1939-) 

*******************************************************************
Corrected by  César Córdova Tapia. (cesar_cordova@yahoo.com)
January 3, 2006.

This is a piece I like very much and realized it had 
several mistakes in the original tab.
I corrected them, added left and right hand fingering, and the 
expression marks that appear in the score.
The fingering is placed under the tab.
The Expression marks are placed above the tab.
You will notice I put different fingers to some single notes that repeat
several times. I found this helpful to remember the times you have to play each
note as you will learn the fingering pattern and not the times each note has
to be played. Anyway, the fingering is only a guide and you can do whatever 
feels more comfortable to your playing.
I found the tempo a little difficult to tab, I will make a midi 
with the correct tempo sometime in the future, meanwhile you can listen
to the recordings of this piece to give you an idea.

Good luck!
César
*******************************************************************
