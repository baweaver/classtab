
Estudio En Imitaciones No.2 - Antonio Lauro (August 3, 1917 – April 18, 1986) 

Here is a beautiful study in imitaion composed by Antonio Lauro, my favorite
composer by far.  This composition is very different from what your use to 
hearing from Lauro, it has a Baroque feel to it.  Listen to the midi for the 
tempo and beat. This song is from the "Quatro Estudios En Imitaciones" I am 
99% sure that its 100% accurate but we all make mistakes.  I have also shown 
left hand fingering as i play it but everyones hand is different.  I hope you
enjoy and appreciate the music as much as i do.

Regards,

Steven Diran Amador
