Notes

I would really recommend listening to the piece before actually trying to
play it, mostly because I found some differences between how it is performed
(by none other than Pepe Romero) and how it is written down on the sheet music.
Some of the most noticeable differences are how the D and A7 chords are
rythmically strummed at the beginnig of the piece. Again, listening to the
piece will help clear up any misunderstandings. And most importantly the piece
is titled Danza Andaluza, in other words it is a dance so it is very lively
(Allegro) and joyful.
