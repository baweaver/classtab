            Title: Helas Madame
           Author: king Henry VIII "Tudor"  (1491 - 1547 / Renaissance)
   Transcribed by: Adam Guzowski (guitarra@poczta.fm)
             Date: 16-IX-2002
Other corrections: (...are welcome)

1) Guitar tuning: (standard) EAGDBE - Old lute music sounds good with Capo
                                      on 3rd or 4th fret

2) General Info: This is another tune from this fabulous composer (and king :)
                 Listen the MIDI. This tune is rather easy, but you will find
                 some tricky parts. Good luck.
      
3) Tablature info: 
    
   1 2 3 4     -> left hand fingering
   (I).....    -> Hold BARRE Chord
   Arp.        -> Arpeggio (all chords can be played with soft arpeggio)
   =====       -> 16th notes
    .          -> Dotted note

Tempo: 4/4   (Aprox. 120 bpm)
