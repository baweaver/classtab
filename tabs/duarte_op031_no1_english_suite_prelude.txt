Prelude from The English Suite (Opus 31) by John W. Duarte (1919-2004)

Tabbed by Richard Finlan, Feb 2003. (last updated 17 Feb 2003)

Duarte arranged a trio of folk songs for the wedding of Andras Segovia. The
Prelude is quite a tricky piece to play, but should present few difficulties
for the average guitarist. It should be played slowly and calmly.

Tempo is a mixture of 3/4, 2/4 and 5/8   32bpm
