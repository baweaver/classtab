Study no. 1
                                       Omaggio a Debussy
Tempo di Giga (Comodo)
===============================================================
This collection is for beginners or children.
This study is composed of short arpeggios (p,i,m) and is for
developing the left hand facility.
Pay attention to the dynamic marking (<<<<< >>>>>).
Maintain a legato (smooth) style.
The tempo is between 100-120 "|." notes/min. (Not too fast.)


Timing is 12/8 and 4/8
