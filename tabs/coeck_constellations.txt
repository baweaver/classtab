*******************************
Constellations, by Armand Coeck 
*******************************

This is a great modern piece showing all the musical versatility from 
composer Armand Coeck; he efficiently managed to use atonality in a 
very meaningful and not unpleasant way. Just add this to your repertoire.
Please watch the video before trying it, the interpretation is great.

Transcribed from the sheet music by Maxime Bergeron (mazout360@hotmail.com).

Video: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4X2RRwrPN6E (Simon Powis)
