*************************************************
Duas Contas, by Garoto (Annibal Augusto Sardinha)
*************************************************

The best of Garoto. This bossa nova influenced many composers such as
A.C.Jobim and Joao Gilberto. Arrangement by Paulo Bellinati (1991).
Transcribed from the sheet music by Maxime Bergeron (mazout360@hotmail.com)

Note: Feel free to experiment with the rhythm. Some of Garoto's works 
such as this piece and Meditacao are meant to be played rubato (at will).
In my opinion, to play this piece with a subtle triplet feel (jazzy mood) 
gives it a very interesting sound.

video: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zuHRkWo4s5E - Paulo Bellinati
video: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OZnzKHj78B8 - Javier de la O

Order of play: measures 1-42, 1-17, 43-75
