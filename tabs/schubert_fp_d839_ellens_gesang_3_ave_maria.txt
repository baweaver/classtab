This well-loved "lied", or song, was written by one of Austria's best-known
Romantic composers. The original is in 4/4 time, in the key of B flat.
But to make the piece easier to sight-read, we've changed it so that sixteenth
notes are now eighth notes. There are some slight modifications in the melodic
rhythm in order to keep the movement legato. If you use a gentle vibrato on
some of the longer melodic notes, they will sustain longer. I use this piece
as an arpeggio study to practise achieving a sweet, mellow tone with the right
hand. I recorded it with Zamfir on "Christmas Dreams"
                                                           Liona Boyd

- LHF = LEFT HAND FINGERING:
    1 = index finger
    2 = medio finger
    3 = ringfinger
    4 = littlefinger

- RHF = RIGHT HAND FINGERING:
    p = thumb
    i = index finger
    m = medio-finger
    a = little-finger/annular

-   "The Pivot-Barré": (as shown above)
    Try to make a "hinge" with your index-finger !
    This will make the sound more fluid with no breaks !
    Meaning holding the 1. finger constantly on the 3.fret/6th string
    in a pivot-movement (barre to non-barre to barre etc)
    This is important in the first 4 measures (the intro).
    (You can also practise the small-repeating section above !)

##############################################################################

                     SYMBOLS/TABLATURE EXPLANATION  

- LHF = LEFT HAND FINGERING:
    1 = index finger
    2 = medio finger
    3 = ringfinger
    4 = littlefinger

- RHF = RIGHT HAND FINGERING:
    p = thumb/pulgar
    i = index finger
    m = medio-finger
    a = little-finger/annular

- Barré = holding 2-6 strings down to fret with
          Left hand 1. finger! (index-finger)
