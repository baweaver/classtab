The Old Lime Tree (Lipa Vekovaia)
Arrangement by Sergei Rudnev (1955-)
Transcribed into tablature by George Dickeson. 2004

=======================================

Notation used (in addition to standard tablature):

 ---5~~~--        Hold note

  ==3==
--5-6-7--         Notes form a triplet

IV                Bar the fourth fret
IV 5/6            Bar top five strings on fourth fret.
Agitato           Play in a restless, agitated manner
Tranquillo        Play in a tranquil manner
Cantabile         Play song-like, melodic, flowing
Sul Ponticello    Pluck strings very close to bridge
                  producing a glassy, high-end sound
Sul Tasto         Pluck strings over the finger board
                  producing a warm, low-end sound  
Accelerando       Gradually increase tempo
Rasgueado Sordino Rub the pad of your plucking hand 
                  index finger rapidly over the strings,
                  emulating a Balalaika
Pizzicato         Right hand muting at the bridge
