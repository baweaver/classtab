*********************************************************
Sonata Romantica III. Allegretto vivo, by Manuel M. Ponce
*********************************************************

Here is an hommage to the great Schubert, composed by M. Ponce for 
Andres Segovia. This is an astonishing and wonderful suite that is,
in my opinion, one of the greatest masterpiece of the classical
guitar repertoire. It is a must-learn for any technically advanced 
guitarist, with its poignant modulations and its composition quality,
worthy of a real Schubert piece.

Some inconsistent fingerings changed, some kept.

Tabbed by Maxime Bergeron (mazout360@hotmail.com).

vid: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ETOQDfb-cd4 - Marcin Dylla (from 12m 26s)
