Notes
-----

Editions used - Berben 1354, Ancona, Italy
                Boije 138 - Haslinger 2113, Vienna
                Hebe a11036 (Geffery) - Tecla 0105, London (2002)

"No. 16 is very similar to a passage at the end of the guitar part of the first
 movement of Giuliani's first concerto in A op. 30."
- from the Preface to the Tecla Edition (Brian Jeffrey, 2006)
http://www.tecla.com/extras/2001/2536/preface.htm

Legend
------

left hand fingering below the tab        right hand fingering above the tab
1 - index finger                         p - thumb
2 - middle finger                        i - index finger
3 - ring finger                          m - middle finger
4 - little finger                        a - annular (ring) finger

=====   - note held
h       - hammer-on
p       - pull-off

 __3__
|  |  | - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes

cII___  - part barre on the 2nd fret using the 1st finer
cV____  - part barre on the 5th fret using the 1st finger
CVII__  - barre on the 7th fret using the 1st finger
cIX___  - part barre on the 9th fret using the 1st finger

f   - forte - loud
sf  - sforzando - strongly accented
