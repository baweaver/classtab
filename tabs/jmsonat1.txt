Sonatina I by Jorge Morel

Tablature by Alex Teixeira (rokstar20@earthlink.net) - August 2002

This is a fun piece, and it flows very nicely from beginning to end. The chords
accentuate the natural resonance of the instrument, and I've found it will make
any guitar sound beautiful. It is the first of the three parts of his Sonatina,
all of which are worth learning.

The piece is in 6/8 time. I did not indicate the duration of the notes, so I
would suggest listening to a recording.

Tuning - DADGBE
