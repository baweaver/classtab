"Dionisio Aguado - New Guitar Method"

An index to the four volume "Dionisio Aguado - Complete works for the guitar"
(Chanterelle) including a cross-reference of all the pieces can be found at:
http://www.donaldsauter.com/dionisio-aguado.htm
