Mauro Giuliani (1781-1829) - The Butterfly

Tabbed by Vincenzo della Vecchia

Here is a nice and easy piece by the great 'Paganini of guitar' Mauro Giuliani.
His deep love for music and the guitar, together with his huge knowledge of the
instrument, led Giuliani to write works of all kinds, from those without any
difficulty, like this, to others which are almost impossible to play.
The notes in this piece are all 1/16, so take care to play it with fluidity;
and don't 'miss' notes in the chord changes.

[last amended 22nd June 2004]
