Notes
-----

Edition used - Oeuvres Pour la Guitare composees par Ferdinand Sor,
Op 60, Introduction a l'Etude de la Guitare,
Frankfurt s/m, Fr. Ph. Dunst PN D. 543 (RiBS)

David Starobin video repeats measures 1-17

Measure 24 - the sheet has upper note of 2nd beat C# (tab and video play C)

Measures 26-32 are a repeat of measures 2-8 with the following differences -
- measure 28 - E (open 6th string) is not tied (held) into measure 29
- measure 29 - the second note in the sheet is D (not B) (tab and video play B)
- measure 32 - D (open 4th string) is dotted (held for an extra half beat)
