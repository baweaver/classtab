Canco del Lladre (Song of the Thief)
Traditional Catalan Folksong  arr. Miguel Llobet (1878-1938)

Tablature by David McGirr <tapsal@freenetname.co.uk> - 9th August, 2000

[amended 16 Aug 2001]
New E-mail: davidmcgirr@hotmail.com

This is a traditional Catalan Folksong which became more popular when
arranged by Miguel Llobet. It is a very beautiful piece and has been 
recorded by many of the greats. It's a very short piece, but I'm sure
you'll love it.

If you would like to hear this piece, I would recommend "Iberia" by
John Williams or "Music of Spain Volume 26" by Julian Bream.

If you find any mistakes please E-mail me.


Key Signature: D major
Time Signature: 4/4
6th string: tuned to D
