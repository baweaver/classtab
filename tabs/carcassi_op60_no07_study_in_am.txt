STUDY No 7 in Am - MATTEO CARCASSI  (Op 60 No 7)

*************************************************************
title info courtesy of Chris Tisdale - 27 September 1998

corrected to MIDI by Weed - weed@wussu.com - 26 December 2001
MIDI on the Classical Guitar Tablature page
*************************************************************

This song is from Noad's '100 Classical Songs for the Guitar', or
something like that. I lent the book to a friend a year ago, haven't
seen it since. The book is not in tabulature, so the conversion is mine.

This is THE best song I know, and it is also easy to play. I've been stealing
songs from the archive for years, figured I had better return the favor.

My first TAB attempt, apologies offered in advance. Give all of the
notes the same time interval [except the G# in line 6 of 2nd part - Weed],
play it moderately fast, and you will figure it out how it is supposed to
sound. Where there are three notes in sequence: (-2-2-2-) the book instructed
the musician to play them (a-m-i) as an exercise.

Mike Yukish
