legend
------

left hand fingering below the tab        right hand fingering above the tab
1 - index finger                         p - thumb
2 - middle finger                        i - index finger
3 - ring finger                          m - middle finger
4 - little finger                        a - annular (ring) finger

===    - note held
<7>    - natural (open) harmonic on the 7th fret
<12>   - natural (open) harmonic on the 12th fret
/      - slide up
\      - slide down
h      - hammer-on
p      - pull-off

cI___  - part barre on the 1st fret with the 1st finger
CI___  - full barre on the 1st fret with the 1st finger
cV___  - part barre on the 5th fret with the 1st finger

gliss. - glissando - a continuous slide from the 1st note to the 2nd note
pizz.  - pizzicato - muted (using outer side of the right hand near the bridge)
