Giuliani's 120 Daily Studies For The Right Hand
Op. 1a
Mauro Giuliani

Edited by Wilson Adkison
wilsonmusic@hotmail.com

In these cornerstone studies, the guitarist discovers a huge booty to 
plunder. The surface yields a great workout to realize right hand dexterity, 
but there is more. Don't let the simple harmony fool you; dig deeper. For 
simplicity sake, Giuliani maintained a consistent harmony of C major with 
the tonic C major moving to its dominant, G7 (in this case a G7/B) and then 
returning to home C major, but there is no reason you should settle in these 
boundaries:

            |------0-------0-------1-------1-------|
            |------1-------1-------3-------3-------|
            |------0-------0-------0-------0-------|
            |------2-------2-------0-------0-------|
            |------3-------3-------2-------2-------|
            |--------------------------------------|

Explore these patterns in scores of harmonic and rhythmic settings.


Giuliani's 120 Daily Studies For The Right Hand
Op. 1a
Mauro Giuliani

Tuning: E A D G B E   -   Time Signatures: 12/8, 4/4
