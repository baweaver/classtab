Legend
------

left hand fingering below tab         right hand fingering above tab
1 - index finger                      p - thumb (pulgar)
2 - middle finger                     i - index finger (indice)
3 - ring finger                       m - middle finger (medio)
4 - little finger                     a - ring finger (anulare, annular)

=====  - note held

arp. - arpeggiate - ripple upwards from the lowest note to the highest note
f - forte - loud
Ferm. - Fermata - notes held for longer than their stated value
cresc. - crescendo - becoming louder
dim. - diminuendo - gradually getting softer
piu - more (piu f - piu forte - loudly)
poco a poco - gradually
pp - pianissimo - very soft
rall. - rallentando - slowing down
sf - sfzorando - forced, strongly accented, sudden loudness

CI____  - barre on the 1st fret with the 1st finger
cV____  - part barre on the 5th fret with the 1st finger
CV____  - barre on the 5th fret with the 1st finger
cVII__  - part barre on the 7th fret with the 1st finger
CVII__  - barre on the 7th fret with the 1st finger
cVIII_  - part barre on the 8th fret with the 1st finger
CVIII_  - barre on the 8th fret with the 1st finger
cIX___  - part barre on the 9th fret with the 1st finger
cX____  - part barre on the 10th fret with the 1st finger
