 The_S: I first heard this piece as a Midi. I know, it is unfortunate.
        But hearing the minor notes and admiring the simplicity of the
        first few bars caused a sort-of addiction for me. I have hunted
        the sheet music throughout the internet, and found only the
        first page, for piano. I later bought the guitar transcription,
        and have now finished converting it into tab form.
        There is one bar which I have outlined with squiggly lines.
        This part you can choose to either play or ignore.
        The recordings I have heard for this song, have this part
        included, but I highlighted it as an optional part because my
        sheet music shows no sign of it.
        Watch the beat measures '|' at the tops of the tabs because
        I have had to space them out occasionally so the tabs are more
        readable.
        Enjoy this fine song, by a fine composer.
