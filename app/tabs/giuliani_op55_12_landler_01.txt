Giuliani's intention seems to be to play all 12 Ländler one after another
without a pause (most of them end not on the tonic, but on the dominant).
If you want to play this Ländler as a stand-alone piece I suggest you
repeat the first section (bars 5-12) once, and then end.

4 - little

p (between notes) - pull off
h (between notes) - hammer on
s (between notes) - slide
