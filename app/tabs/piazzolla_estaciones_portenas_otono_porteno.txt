Duration Legend
---------------
H - half note
Q - quarter note
E - 8th note
S - 16th note
T - 32nd note
X - 64th note
. - dotted note - time and a half
|-3-|  - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
|-6-|  - hextuplet - 6 notes played in the time of 4 notes
|-9-|  - nonuplet - 9 notes played in the time of 8 notes

Tablature Legend
----------------
h  - hammer on
L  - tied note
p  - pull off
s  - slide
tr - trill
\  -  slide into from above or out of downwards
^  - brush up
v  - brush down

NH   - natural harmonic
<9>  - natural harmonic on the open string, 9th fret
<12> - natural harmonic on the open string, 12th fret
<24> - natural harmonic on the open string, 24th fret
