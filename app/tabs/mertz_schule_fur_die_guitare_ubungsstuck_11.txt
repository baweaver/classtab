This is an exact tab from the original edition. However i think that
the last 0 on the first string in bar 8 should only be played the second
time around. Similarly in bar 16, the first time i play 0 on the first
string on the last beat of the bar, and the second time around i play it
as written below. You can hear these changes in my YouTube video.
