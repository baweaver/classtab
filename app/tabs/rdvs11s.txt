Key Signature: A minor [transposed from Bm]
Tempo: Andantino
Publisher's note:
This Sarabande is part of Suite XI by Robert de Visee.
Accurately observe the timing of the notes, and watch chord density.
Watch the polyphony in the 2nd part very closely, especially in the
left hand where it is more technically complex. Be careful in apoyando,
(rest strokes) making sure you don't mute neighbouring strings.
