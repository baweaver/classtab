It is necessary to be precise about the timing of this graceful and quite easy
study. The polonaise, or polacca in Italian, is a typical Polish dance, in 3/4,
in the time of andante mosso; so you can play this piece using just this timing.

See my tab of Giuliani's Variazioni op.112 if you need information about the
musical terms and synbols used.

MAURO GIULIANI (BISCEGLIE 1781 - NAPOLI 1829)

Signature: A major
Tuning: standard
