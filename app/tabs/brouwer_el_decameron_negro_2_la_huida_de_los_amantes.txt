El Decameron Negro - 2. La Huida De Los Amantes Por El Valle De Los Ecos
(La Fuite des Amants par la Vallee des Echos)
By Leo Brouwer

Tabbed by Scott Erickson (axeman1033@aol.com)

See key at bottom for notes on techniques.

This is a great song, but extremely difficult.  The time signature
changes almost every measure.  The timing for most of the song is 
really loose, there are a lot of held notes.  The time signatures
are not very strict, except in the Echo Melody part.

Key
---

h hammer on
p pull off
<7> natural harmonic

RHF
p-thumb
i-index
m-middle
a-ring

Dynamics
fff-very very loud
ff -very loud
f  -loud
mf -medium loud
mp -medium soft
p  -soft
pp -very soft
ppp-very very soft
p> -play soft and fade volume
cresc.> - get louder

Tempos
Presto   -very very fast
Moderato -medium pace
Slow     -slow
rit.>    -get slower

---^---
---4---  - fermata, hold note longer than its value
---*---

--(4)--  - strike string with thumb 
--(0)--    to get a percussive sound
