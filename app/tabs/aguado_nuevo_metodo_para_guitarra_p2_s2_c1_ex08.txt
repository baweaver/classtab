Notes
-----

The tab is based on the Benito Campo 1843 edition.
Left hand fingering based on the Thorlaksson 2000 edition.

Aguado's wrote six 'Methods' often recycling the same lessons and exercises.
For info on three of the methods see - "Dionisio Aguado - New Guitar Method"
Introduction to the Tecla edition (Brian Jeffery, 1981)
http://www.tecla.com/extras/0001/0011/0011intro.htm

4th beat is E (not G) -
