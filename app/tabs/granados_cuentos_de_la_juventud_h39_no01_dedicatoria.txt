Cuentos de la Juventud (Scenes of Childhood) - Enrique Granados (1867-1916)
Op 1 H.39 - 10 Pieces for Piano - No 1 Dedicatoria

This is probably one of the most beautiful pieces in the whole guitar
repertoire. It is not too difficult but may take some time to get the 
poetical quality and serenity across successfully.  Let all the notes
ring when possible. I added some left hand fingerings for the 3rd bar
because it might seem ambiguous since the G note on the 4th string
should sustain for the whole bar.

[This is a 'shorter' arrangement of the piece.]

KEY SIGNATURE: D major
TUNING: Drop the 6th string to D
TIME SIGNATURE: 2/4 but it uses 8th note triplets throughout thus           
                creating the illusion of 6/8 time.
TEMPO: Andantino, Play it flowingly with an easy tempo
