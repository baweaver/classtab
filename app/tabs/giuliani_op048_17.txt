Notes
-----

Editions used - Berben 1354, Ancona, Italy
                Boije 138 - Haslinger 2113, Vienna
                Hebe a11037 (Jeffery) - Tecla 0105, London (2002)
                Kruger (1906)
                Thorlaksson 1080 (2001)

In the C18th, "Andantino" meant slower than Andante, (today it usually means
faster), but the piece is sometimes played much faster. 

The Berben edition transposes much of the piece higher up the guitar -

Legend
------

left hand fingering below the tab
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

====   - note held
h      - hammer-on
p      - pull-off

cI__   - part barre on the 1st fret

> > >  - decrescendo - getting softer
dolce  - sweetly, gently, expressively
f      - forte - loud
ff     - fortissimo - very loud
mf     - mezzo forte - medium loud
p      - piano - soft
sf     - sforzando - strongly accented
