Subject: J.S. Bach --Bouree from Partita No 1 for solo violin BWV 1002
Johann Sebastian Bach
Bouree from Partita No. 1 for Solo Violin BVW 1002

Submitted by Rod Kahler (browsers@flash.net)

I really like this song. It is in 4/4 timing. Look out for running 8th
notes. The Bouree is a brisk, lively dance, so make sure to beat the hell
out of this one.

You may want to listen to this song. You can hear on the Segovia CD
"an all Bach performance", or at the Classical Midi Archives (www.prs.net)
