Legend
------

left hand fingering below the tab
1 - index
2 - middle
3 - ring
4 - little

======   held note
p        pull-off
h        hammer-on
AH       artificial harmonic (play on fret, touch string for octave harmonic)
NH       natural harmonic (play open string, touch string at the fret shown)

< - - .  crescendo (getting louder)
. - - >  decrescendo (getting softer)
.        staccato (above the tab - measures 29-31)
<        accent (stressed)

attacca  move on to the next movement (variation) without a pause
dim.     diminuendo (becoming quieter)
f        forte (loud)
ff       fortissimo (very loud)
mf       mezzo forte (medium loud)
non      not, do not (non dim. - do not get quieter)
p        piano (soft)
pp       pianissimo (very soft)
piu      more (piu f - piu forte - louder)
poco     little (poco a poco - little by little)
rit.     ritenuto (slowing down)
sfz      sforzando (strongly stressed)
sub.     subito (suddenly - sub. p - suddely softer)

cI____   part barre on the 1st fret
cIII__   part barre on the 3rd fret
cV____   part barre on the 5th fret
CVI___   barre on the 6th fret
CVII__   barre on the 7th fret
CVIII_   barre on the 8th fret
CIX___   barre on the 9th fret
cX____   part barre on the 10th fret
cXI___   part barre on the 11th fret
cXII__   part barre on the 12th fret
