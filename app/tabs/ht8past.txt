            Title: Pastime
           Author: King Henry VIII "Tudor" (1491-1547 / Renaissance)
   Transcribed by: Adam Guzowski (guitarra@poczta.fm)
             Date: 14-IX-2002
Other corrections: (...are welcome)

1) Guitar tuning: (standard) EAGDBE - Old lute music sounds good with Capo on
                  3rd or 4th fret

2) General Info: Chords, chords, chords. All about chords. Hope the LHF
                 will solve the problem. Keep the tempo. Listen to the MIDI
                 This one is not really for beginners, or maybe it is... :)
                 In other transcriptions this tune should be played twice, 
                 like it is in the MIDI file.
                 Good luck.

   Arp.        -> Arpeggio
   Stc.        -> Staccato
   =====       -> 16th notes
    .          -> Dotted note
    x          -> short pause (8th pause)


Tempo:  4/4  (aprox. 90/100 bpm)
