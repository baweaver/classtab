 ------

 | - quarter note
 ._
 |  -  staccato

 XI________ - barre

 1/2 II____ - barre on the treble strings only

 arm. - natural harmonics

 [pp] - expression

vivace - very fast
subito = suddenly (it pertains to reprise in this piece)
D.C. al Coda - start plaing piece from the beginning to the
coda sign, then scip to coda.
poco - a little, rather
rit. - getting gradually slower
pizz. - plucked
