Title: Menus Propos Enfantins (Infantile Remarks)
I - Le Chant Guerrier Du Roi Des Haricots (Warrior Song of the King of Beans)
Composer: Erik Satie (1866-1925)
Transcription and Tab by: Raul M.H. Bonde (b.1980) <montyhast@yahoo.com>
R = quarter rest
All three songs of Menus Propos Enfantins should be played together as part of
the same piece. The other two parts are -
II - Ce Que Dit La Petite Princesse Des Tulipes (What the Little Princess of
Tulips Said)
III - Valse Du Chocolat Aux Amandes (Waltz of the Chocolate Almonds)
