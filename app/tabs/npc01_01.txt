Niccolo Paganini - 1st Caprice in E Major, & G Major {Opus.1,#1}
(Arr. Eliot Fisk)

Transcribed by: Jon Clark

Most of the passages in this song are done by using a very common technical
excercise, and you'll definitely reap benfits from learning it. Some parts
are incredibly difficult, and I've tried to make things as straight forward
as possible. The big problem is the distances between some notes, and the
height of some of the arpeggios. I might add that most of the notes are 32nds,
so don't let the slow tempo mislead you. The midi file has been slowed down
as well in order to make it a little easier to comprehend. If you have any
questions or comments feel free to E-mail me at furgitarre@hotmail.com

Have fun, and Good Luck, you'll need it hehe.

[last revised 31 January 2003]
