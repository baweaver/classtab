This intense study, entirely built on pimami arpeggio, requires a flexible
right hand, because arpeggios are fast, and here speed is essential, 'cause
this is one of those pieces that lose all their fascination if played slowly.
Select Courier font to view the tab, and if you need to, see my Variazioni
Op. 112 tab in order to find other explanations about musical notation.

The MIDI for this study (created by F.Faucher) is simplified - in the sheet
music, measures 19 to the last but one are the same as 11-18 - so the MIDI
plays measure 18 and then jumps directly to the last measure.

MAURO GIULIANI (BISCEGLIE 1789 - NAPOLI 1821)

Tempo : 4/4
Tonalità : Mi minore (E minor)
Accordatura : Standard (EBGDAE)
Data : Venerdì 5 luglio 2004 (7/5/2004)
