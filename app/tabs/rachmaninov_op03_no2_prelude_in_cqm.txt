Fellow guitarist, here is a transposition of one of Rachmaninoff's best known
preludes.  All though it is very difficult, the chords and techniques involved
in playing this piece can teach one to understand and appreciate the guitar's
potential more fully.  I hope you enjoy!


PRELUDE IN C# MINOR (OPUS 3, NO 2) - RACHMANINOFF (1873-1943)
