Miller's Dance                                  

                                                                 Manuel DeFalla

This is the solo version of the popular Defalla piece. It is in high voice and
is a bit different from the way it is usually performed (but only at the end).

Play fast but don't follow the midi exactly, it tends to exaggerate a bit.

Tabbed by: Zeljko Matovic

Tuning - EADGBE
