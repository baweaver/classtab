The Seventh Caprice in A Minor, and A Major. (Opus#1, #7)
By:Niccolo Paganini - Arranged for guitar by:Eliot Fisk
Transcribed by:Jon Clark

Here's a wonderful tune which is loaded with lots of fun scale work,
and sadistic chord changes. For those who aren't familiar with music
theory the (*pont.*) stands for ponticello, which simply means play
(pluck) near the bridge (not to close though) Enough to make a sharp
contrast to the regular dark tone which is ever so prevalent in the
classical guitar. (*ord.*) stands for ordinario, which simply means
"usual" or the way that you normally play stuff. Eliot Fisk plays it
around double to triple that of the midi file, which I would guess is
around 90-100 beats per minute.

Have fun, and good luck!
