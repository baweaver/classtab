Notes and Legend
----------------
The time signature of the sheet music is 3/4 (to emphasise that it is a waltz),
but the sheet music is written as though it were 3/8, which usually means that
the piece is to be played at a fast rather than a slow tempo. The tempo of the
Norbert Neunzling video is 108 bpm (eighth beats).

measure 9 - the last note (D) is played staccato (detached, cut short)

=     - note held
g     - grace note (played as pull offs where possible)
h     - hammer on
p     - pull off
cII_  - partial barre on the 2nd fret with the 1st finger
