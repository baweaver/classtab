from the Well-Tempered Clavier, Book I, by J.S. Bach

Tabbed by Nick Choly
choly@fas.harvard.edu

I had to fudge a few notes by an octave here and there, but for the
most part, it holds to the original.
With the vertical bars | delimiting beats, I recommend striving for
100 beats per minute.
