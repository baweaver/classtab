Courante
Santino Garsi (1542-1604)

All I know is, that Santino Garsi was a luthenist and composer from 
Parma, Italia. This Courante, or Corrente in Italian, is a nice and 
very easy piece. A courante is a rather fast ternary dance from the 
Renaissance and Baroque eras.
I made this tab by ear, so it is possible that some of the notes are
missing or superfluous. 
Please send your remarks or suggestions to: nenuok@yahoo.fr

François Joseph Kuonen, 03.22.2010.

3/4
~170 beats per minute
* = let ring just one beat
Play each part twice, and then finish by playing the 1st part once.
