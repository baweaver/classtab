Op 35 No 22 in Bm (Segovia Study No 5) - Fernando Sor (1778-1839)

Date: Mon, 25 Dec 95 11:39 PST
From: rainbow@chatlink.com (Michael and Victor Zefas)
Subject: classical guitar song by Fernando Sor: "Estudio 5 in Bm"

"Here is study #5 of the famous Twenty. It is in the key of Bm and is in 3/4
time. Generally, each pluck of the string is an eighth note. On the recording
I have, the first note of almost every measure is held just a little longer
and usually there should be an accentuation (rest stroke) on the first note of
each measure. This song, also, needs to be played slow and almost hesitatingly.
This is truly a most beautiful piece. I have been using OLGA a lot lately but
haven't given anything. So I gave this, my first submission to OLGA. This piece
needs to be played with a lot of expression.  If played with it, this piece
becomes soul-touching. Enjoy." - Victor Zefas of Rogue River, Oregon
