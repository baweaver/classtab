********************************************************
Aquarela do Brasil - Ary Barroso (arr. C. Barbosa-Lima)
********************************************************

Great, well known song from Ary Barroso. This is the best arrangement
that I could find for this one. Tabbed by Maxime Bergeron 
(mazout360@hotmail.com)

* Notes : the ending should be played as followed: left hand thumb for the
lower notes (yes, awkward hand position indeed) and the harmonics are 
played with a barré over the 19th fret. Personally, I use my index for the 
bass which gives a similar result. Feel free to play the 12th fret as an 
harmonic if it seems easier.

video: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JvVUk_jn09w (Carlos Barbosa-Lima)

Legend below the tab

tuning: DADGBE    key: D   time: 4/4   tempo: 100 bpm (half notes) (see video)

The first 6 lines is free play.
