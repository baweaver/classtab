The Third Caprice in E-Minor (Opus#1, #3)
By:Niccolo Paganini - Arranged for guitar by:Eliot Fisk
Tabbed By:Jon Clark

This one has a bizarre technique in the first section known as a
double trill. I can't say I've seen it anywhere else except
some of the other caprices by Paganini.
Since the definition of trill is simply a rapid alternation
between 2 notes, there is nothing that says a trill can't be
played on 2 different strings.

I'm sure there are better left hand fingerings in some spots
so don't be afraid to experiment, as they're not written in
stone. The midi file has been slowed right down, and there
are no double trills included in it, as my scoring program
doesn't seem to like trills. If you have any questions or
comments don't hesitate to E-mail me at:
       Furgitarre@hotmail.com

Good luck!
