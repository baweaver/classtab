Study n.15 is without doubt one of the funniest (and one of the hardest to
play) of opus 48. Giuliani uses in a manner sufficiently wide the resources
of the guitar: legature ascendenti and discendenti, little runs, arpeggios,
appoggiature; all this without losing that beauty of the sound that
characterizes all his works. After all, this is his greatness.

Like I do in all my tabs, I've included the composer's indications
(f, mf, crescendo etc); even if they are often neglected by the MIDIs,
I suggest you pay attention to them and respect them: this certainly
will be beneficial to your execution.

If you have any questions or suggestions, or if you find any errors in the
tab, feel free to e-mail me at the address above. Buon divertimento!

MAURO GIULIANI (BISCEGLIE 1781 - NAPOLI 1829)
