Originally submitted to Weed's Classical Guitar Tablature page

=============================================================

Notes:

Here's a real gem by de Murcia - the "Final" in particular
being the reason I had to tab this piece. I apologize for
the strange time signatures and quantization...it just
happened that way, and it felt like too much work to change.
The left hand fingering is no problem, so I didn't bother
with it - the challenge is rather for the right hand, in
the "Final."

Tabbed from a recording of a superb performance by Jose
Maria Gallardo del Rey. A million thanks to Ronimusic
(www.ronimusic.com) for releasing the "Amazing Slow Downer"
for MacOSX, without which I could never have tabbed the
"Final" so accurately!

Comments and corrections welcome!
