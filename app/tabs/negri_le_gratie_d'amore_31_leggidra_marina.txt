Le Gratie d'Amore - 31. Leggiadra Marina - Cesare Negri (1536-1605)

edited by: Bart T. (bla_1024@hotmail.com), 10/2014

"La Musica della sonata con l'Intavolatura di liuto del Ballo Leggiadra Marina.
 Le prime due parti si fanno due volte per parte, la gagliarda si fa due volte,
 & due volte la sciolta, poi si torna à far la prima parte due volte, e la 
 gagliarda due volte la prima parte, e si finisce'l ballo."

2x part I, 2x part II, 2x part III, 2x part IV, 2x part V,
2x part I, 2x part II, 3x part III, 2x part IV, 2x part I.

Please, note the alternative repeat signs in measures 4, 8, 20 and 26.
Renaissance music was often composed in phrases without the strict time notation
that we are used to now. I decided to write the music  down in phrases too. This
lead to the alternative repeat signs in bars 4, 8, 20 and 26, where the upbeats
of bars 5 and 21 are included within the repeat signs. When playing this dance,
the first notes of the bars should remain in a steady rhythm though.
