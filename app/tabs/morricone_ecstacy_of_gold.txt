=====
Notes
=====
Ecstacy of Gold was composed by Ennio Morricone for the movie
"The Good, the Bad and the Ugly" directed by Sergio Leone.
A great song that has a Romance de Espana plucking feel. Plucking exercise with
arpeggios. A couple of barre chords: B Major on the 2nd fret and 7th fret. Pluck
the song uniformly in mostly pimi pattern. There are times you will need to play
the same string twice with same finger. You may want to use pull offs instead to
keep plucking smoother. You can undulate the volume for emotions. Keep in tempo
(better to go slow than fast and lose the beat). I finished the song with
harmonics on the 12 fret. Equally nice would be Em Chord strum.

======
Legend
======
Left                       Right                 
p = pull off               p = pulgar = thumb    
h = hammer on              i = indice = index     
/ = slide                  m = medio = middle     
= = suspend/hold Note      a = annular = ring      
V = Barre at fret 5       
Poco rit. = slow down gradually
