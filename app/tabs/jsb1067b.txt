Badinerie (from Orchestral suite No. 2 in B Minor) BWV 1067
J. S. Bach (1685-1750)

Tabbed by Pearl (boinarage@aol.com) - last amended 10 December 2002

This is exactly the kind of thing that I stumble over the most. It's fast and 
furious, so you don't get any extra time to relax, and the position changes 
and inside-out fingering always throw me. On the other hand, it's a truly 
wonderful piece. Put in the time to get it flowing, which should be easier in 
this transcription to A Minor (lots of open strings).

If you find this sort of piece easy, please don't ever tell me. Some things 
that many people call very difficult come easily to me, too, but I promise 
not to rub your nose in it.

Enjoy!
