Minuet in C - Augustin Barrios Mangore (1885-1944) - Arr. Lawrence Ferrara

Here is a cool little minuet from the Classical Guitar Master from Paraguay.
This arrangement is from Lawrence Ferrara's book "The Student Repertoire
Series Vol. 2". I highly recommend this book to any serious guitarist.
By the way, if you have this book and would like any of the pieces tabbed,
let me know because I have tabbed pretty much all of the pieces (on paper).
It would just be a matter of me prying my hands away from my guitar and
applying them to my PC to type them.  Anyway, the offer has been made.

Tab by David Maurice Garrett - Fourfox@earthlink.net
