                                  O ASTRONAUTA
                 Music by Baden Powell & Vinicius de Moraes

here's a favourite bossa of mine!  this sweet little song is very fun to play, 
you can muck around with the rhythms a lot.  and it's much easier to play than 
it sounds.  as usual in bossa, mute the bassline slightly with the right-hand 
palm, and pluck all the chords with i.m.a. (no strumming!)  ..have fun!  :)
