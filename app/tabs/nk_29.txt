left-hand fingering (below the tab)             CI    - barre on 1st fret
   1     - 1st (fore) finger                    CII   - barre on 2nd fret
   2     - 2nd (index) finger                   CIII  - barre on 3rd fret
   3     - 3rd (ring) finger                    CIV   - barre on 4th fret
   4     - 4th (little) finger (pinkie)         CV    - barre on 5th fret
                                                CVI   - barre on 6th fret
   =     - note held                            CVII  - barre on 7th fret
  ///    - slide up                             CVIII - barre on 8th fret
  \\\    - slide down                           CIX   - barre on 9th fret
   h     - hammer on
   p     - pull off
  arp.   - ripple from bass up
  <12>   - natural (open string) harmonic on 12th fret (etc)
 1<13>   - artificial harmonic, left hand 1st fret, harmonic on 13th fret
 3<15>   - art. harmonic, left hand 3rd fret, harmonic on 15th fret (etc)
___3___  - triplet (3 notes in the time of 2)

bars 2-5 - if u can't manage the harmonics, just play the normal notes
