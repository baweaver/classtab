Milonga Del Angel (Tango)
by Astor Piazzolla

This is a masterpiece by Piazzolla, Milonga Del Angel.
It sounds very very great, beautiful and graceful, just
like the Tango by Albeniz, but a little bit ..sentimental.
  
You may also hear this played by AL DI MEOLA at -
http://www.piazzolla.org/music/imm1-11.ra

Should you have any questions mail me seadrop@hongkong.com
