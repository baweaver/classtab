Sakura (Japanese Folk Song)

If you've listened to the Sakura Variations by Yuquiliro Yocoh,
this is the theme of the variations. It's a simple melody.
No doubt, it's the most popular and best known Japanese folk song.
And it's very suitable for the beginner :-)
So I also added the LHF and the midi too. Good luck ~
