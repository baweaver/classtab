Leo Brouwer - Suite No 2 (Suite in D) Preludio

Here's my attempt at writing some tab. I scored this from the sheet music
(special thanks to Weed) so it should be fairly accurate, but if you see any
errors or have any suggestions please let me know.

Performance notes:
As with any work, you'll have your own interpretation, but in general: Be sure
the high melody rings out clearly, while with the lower melody you may want to
slight accelerate and decelerate to give it more of a 'living' feel to it.
Watch out for the bar of 5/4 and there is ample use of barring throughtout the
song. I choose a time of 65 bpms based off a recording from the CD "Latin
Guitar festival".

Corrections/Comments: please send to jmeier@cox.net
