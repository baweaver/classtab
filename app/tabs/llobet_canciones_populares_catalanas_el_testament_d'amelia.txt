El testament D'Amelia by Miguel Llobet
--------------------------------------

This piece has drop D tuning.

Square brackets denote artificial harmonics. To play these you must
fret the indicated note while simultaneously plucking and stopping
the string at a point 12 frets higher. Angled brackets are natural
harmonics.
