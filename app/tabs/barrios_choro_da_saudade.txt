Choro da Saudade (Nostalgic Choro)
Author: Agustin Barrios Mangore (1885-1944)
Transcription by: Gianpiero Ciammaricone
                  gianpiero@mindless.com
                  http://www.geocities.com/vienna/6619

Time Signature: 2/4

Tuning: DGDGBE
        To tune your guitar this way easily, make the 6th string
        sound like the 4th string (Octave) and the 5th string
        sound like the 3rd string (Octave)

This is a really hard piece, specially for the long stretches done
in some parts. For example in part 2 you have to play a chord in
the 6th fret with the bass in the 1st fret (Look at it to see what
I mean)
                          Good luck and enjoy!!!!!!
