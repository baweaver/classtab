SUITE IN A MINOR - Francisco Corbetta (1615 - 1681)

Arranged and tabbed by Gavin Emery (gavinemery@aol.com)

Francisco Corbetta was the most renowned guitarist of his era - Gaspar Sanz 
described him as "the best of all". He was born in Italy and died in France, and
between 1639 and 1674 he published at least 6 books for guitar (one now lost).
He travelled all over Europe, taught and performed for the royalty of several
countries, and was probably the first ever "guitar hero".
