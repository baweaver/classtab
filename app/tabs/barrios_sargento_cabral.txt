Sargento Cabral by Agustin Barrios  (1885-1944)

Tabbed by Jake Hart (fuzz54@yahoo.com)
Date:  1/17/04

mp3 available from http://www.jakoloco.com/music.htm

This piece was written in honor of a soldier in  
Argentina's army who saved the life of General San 
Martin by using his body to shield the General from
an enemy bayonet.  He died in the arms of the General. 
The people of Argentina raised him to the rank of 
sergeant after his death.
