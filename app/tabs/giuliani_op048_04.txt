Legend
------

Moderato - at a moderate pace (108-120 bpm)
forte - loud

 .   - staccato (the note is cut short, held for less than it's value)
 h   - hammer-on
 p   - pull-off
===  - note held

1 - index
2 - middle
3 - ring
