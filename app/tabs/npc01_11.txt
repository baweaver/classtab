This is a slower caprice by Paganini, from his 24 Caprices. It is also
one of the more beautiful sounding, in my opinion. The tempo is slow,
and I have only transcribed the first slow section. A second, faster
section follows this one, which is then followed by the slow section
again to finish the caprice. I  have included chords below the TAB,
and comments are on the top.
