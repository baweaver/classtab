Eine Kleine Nachtmusik (A Little Night Music) K525 - 1st movement
- Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

What follows is one of my favourite confections for strings. I've never seen
a guitar arrangement of this, so I thought it was about time to make one.
I haven't set all of the fingerings in stone yet, but I think that most of
it is pretty sound. I've chosen to omit any ornamentation (trills, turns,
mordents etc...) to make the arrangement more playable.
E-mail me any ideas/suggestions.

Transcribed and arranged by Courtney Babbidge (cbm7b5@yahoo.com)
13 Dec 2002


4/4 Time
Allegro
